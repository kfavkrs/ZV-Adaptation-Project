# Adaptation Blueprint — Roadmap

**Status:** Approved 2026-05-28
**Scope:** Research phase only. Produces the evidence-backed framework document that stories, missions, and gamification will be designed from in a future phase.
**Deliverable:** `adaptation-research-framework.md` — a complete, sourced, spec-ready research document.

---

## Slices

| Slice | Title | Status | Risk | Depends | Description |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| S01 | Framework Foundation & Temporal Arc | complete | medium | — | Establishes the composite theoretical framework (BMO, Ward's ABC, Kim's spiral, Oberg's U-Curve) and the full emotional arc of adaptation with stage-by-stage timeline markers. The structural spine everything else hangs on. |
| S02 | Core Life Domains — Social, Emotional, Linguistic | complete | high | S01 | Deep research treatment of the three highest-friction domains for Americans in the Netherlands — the ones most likely to determine whether the move feels like it's working in year one. |
| S03 | Extended Life Domains — Practical, Professional, Pet | complete | medium | S01 | Covers the seven remaining domains: healthcare, housing, financial, legal/civic, professional/purpose, pet logistics, and daily life. More factual and system-oriented than S02, but equally essential to the full picture. |
| S04 | Integration, Pre-Move Teaser & Spec Readiness | complete | low | S02, S03 | Synthesizes cross-domain patterns, adds a light pre-move primer for the weeks before departure, and ensures the full document is structured so a game/product designer could use it as a spec without this conversation. |

---

## Definition of Done (per domain)

- At least one academic source and one experiential source
- US-to-NL cultural delta embedded in the narrative (not siloed)
- Temporal markers — when the domain peaks in difficulty, mapped to adaptation stages
- Couple framing — late 40s, no kids, two small dogs, adjusting together but not always in sync

## Definition of Done (full document)

- All 10 life domains covered
- Cross-domain synthesis section written
- Pre-move teaser section written
- Document is self-contained — a designer unfamiliar with this conversation could spec a gamified system from it

---

## Out of Scope (this phase)

- Gamification design, bingo mechanics, or quest/mission structure
- Utrecht-specific local resource mapping (cafes, meetup groups, vet clinics)
- Children / schooling domains
- Software or app development
